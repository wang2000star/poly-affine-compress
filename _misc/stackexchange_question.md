**Minimizing ANF monomials via affine input substitution**

Given $f: \mathbb{F}_2^n \to \mathbb{F}_2$ in ANF, let $T(f)$ be its number of monomials. Find $M \in \mathbb{F}_2^{m \times n}, b \in \mathbb{F}_2^m$ and $g$ with $f(x) = g(Mx \oplus b)$ such that $T(g) < T(f)$.

Are there any known heuristic algorithms for minimizing $T(g)$ in this setting?
